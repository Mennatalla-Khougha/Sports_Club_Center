World Squash Federation (WSF) Egyptian Squash Association

Squash is a fast-paced, high-intensity racket sport played by two players in a four-walled court with a small, hollow rubber ball. The objective of the game is to hit the ball in such a way that the opponent is not able to return it. Squash is a great workout that can help you burn calories, improve your cardiovascular health, and build muscle.
